1971 Championship

1971 Bucks first championship ever. Playing the Lakers in conference finals and winning 4-1. Playing against 4 hall of famers.
While bucks had 3 hall of famers. Efter they won the conf finals they met bullets in the finals where they met 3 hall of famers.
They won 4-0 in the finals with Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, Oscar Robertson and Bob Dambridge. Kareem averaged 27 points, 18,5 rebounds and 2.8 assists per game.
Oscar averaged 23.5 points, 5 rebounds and 9.5 assists. Bob averaged 20.3 points, 9.8 rebounds and 3.3 assists.
With all of them carrying their team to victory and some roleplayers playing well averaging 10 points per game.
The one who won most valuable player was kareem who averaged 34,.8 points, 16,6 rebounds and 4,6 assists. He also won finals mvp.

2021 Championship

2021 bucks won their second championship after 50 years of trying to get it again. The one who was the face of that team was Giannis Antetokounmpo, Khris middelton, Jrue Holiday, Brook Lopez, Pat Connaughton. With Giannis being the best player on the team and winning finals MVP when averaged 35 points, 13 rebounds, 5 assist.
Everyone was doing their part. Playing 6 games with the finals score being 4-2 with bucks winning over hall of famer crhis Paul and future hall of famer Devin booker.
